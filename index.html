What is Greenden

Inspired by a lifetime love for plants and plant-related knowledge, Ship My Plants offers a uniquely innovative, fair, and open marketplace for local plant buyers and sellers alike.

About ship my plants

Before, there wasn't that one, go to place that plant and garden enthusiasts could go and find just about anything they were looking for all in one place… until now! Inspired by a lifetime love for plants.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be Earth’s most plant and people-centric company, and to create a fair and open marketplace for people who love plants. Buyers will find a spot to explore and discover new plants, old favorites.

History

For over 5 decades and two generations the founders of Ship My Plants have belonged to the garden center industry - from plant production to brick and mortar retail, and most recently, the online selling experience.
